Projects

  • Final Project

An effective controller design for BLDC motor drive with Giant Trevally Optimizer

In this project, an effective controller design for the BLDC motor drive is proposed using nature inspired Giant Trevally Optimizer (GTO). The PI controller is developed for the speed control of BLDC motor using Giant Trevally Optimizer. The gain settings of a PI controller are improved using GTO. The dynamic characteristics of the BLDC motor are observed by the developed model using MATLAB/Simulink environment. The suggested controller’s performance is evaluated under a variety of load and set speed settings, and it is compared to other known optimization approaches such as PSO. Based on the simulation results, it is clear that the suggested controller performs better under all of the drive’s operating conditions.
